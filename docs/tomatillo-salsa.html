3.5 Tomatillo Salsa

Alicia Ebert

3.5.1 Pitch

My good friend from Arizona made the best salsa and I finally got the recipe (shhhh).

3.5.2 Materials

  • ½ lb tomatillos
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp cilantro
  • ½ cup white or sweet onion
  • 1 medium jalapeno
  • 2 small banana peppers

3.5.3 Methods

  1. Blend all ingredients in a blender.
  2. You can roast the tomatillos until softened as well.
