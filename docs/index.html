Our Mission

This cookbook is a compilation of The University of Vermont Biology Department’s favorite holiday recipes.

One of the best parts of the holiday season is often sitting around a table, enjoying food together, and sharing the personal stories that are behind each recipe. This year we can’t come together in person, but we can still share those stories, and hopefully spread some holiday cheer in the form of good food!

We hope this cookbook is an opportunity for us to get to know each other a little better. It is a place where we can share the meaningful stories behind our favorite recipes, and try delicious new recipes.

You may submit new recipes on this google form.
